What is the Toy Foundation?

“The Toy Foundation™ is a 501 (c)(3) Nonprofit children's charity. Our mission is to be the uniting force for the collective philanthropy of the toy industry for the benefit of all children in need.”

Research Conducted

    1. 1-on-1 Interviews with 5 different subjects
    2. Online Survey with 9 responses
    3. 1-on-1 Usability Tests with 5 subjects
    4. Competitor Analysis between Toy foundation, Gofundme, and Salvation army
    5. A/B testing
    Objective 1: We want to understand donating habits.
    Objective 2: We want to understand how to increase donations.
    Objective 3: We want to understand what is important information donors like to know about an organization.

Key Takeaways from Research

    1. People need to understand how their money is being used 
    2. People need to understand the goal and mission before donating s
    3. Most people prefer donating online and like the option to remain anonymous subjects

Problem Statement

The Toy Foundation's current webpage was designed as a charitable platform that seeks donations to ensure children in need have access to toys and uplifting experiences. Which in turn ideally leads to a better quality of life for these afflicted children in their developmental years. However, we have  observed that the current design could better inform potential donors and include an quicker and easier donation process. How might we improve the design so that donors feel equipped with information that feels trustworthy a before agreeing to contribute financially?

Things to Improve

Donors should have an easy way to access information such as the foundations purpose and goals before donating

Donation process needs to be quick and easy so that the user doesn’t leave the site before completing the task of donating

The original toy foundation web page was lacking a playful and fun aesthetic despite being a toy drive for children

Solution

Prioritize important information, such as past impact, mission, and goals, by including it on the homepage.

Make Donation button bold and interactive to make it the most important thing on the page

Redesign Donation page to be quick and painless with multiple checkout options

Redesign all pages to convey playfulness and reflect a toy drive
